Install Sales360

Installation Instructions:

  1. Tap the "Install Sales360" button above
  2. If prompted, tap "Install" in the popup dialog
  3. Go to Settings > General > VPN & Device Management
  4. Find your enterprise developer certificate and tap "Trust"
  5. The app will now be available on your home screen
Note: This app is distributed for enterprise use only. You may need to trust the developer certificate in your device settings.
